Colorado Custom Wraps

Colorado Springs, CO

At Colorado Custom Wraps, we specialize in Vinyl and Plasti-dip application. We use the finest 3M products to ensure every job is done right the first time. Whether you are looking for graphic printed vinyl for business advertising, carbon fiber vinyl, solid colors or chrome. We wrap and/or dip:

  • Cars
  • Trucks
  • Trailers
  • Atv's
  • Motorcycles
  • Side-by-sides
  • Rims
  • Bicycles
  • And more!

Big or small, we wrap and paint them all! Hands down the the one stop shop for vinyl and Plasti-dip applications and Plasti-dip products.
